Purpose Unlimited Inc.

Based on the search results, here are the summaries of news articles concerning Purpose Unlimited Inc. from the past 30 days (March 6, 2025 - April 5, 2025):

1. Acquisition of Steadyhand Investment Management (March 24, 2025)

  • Purpose Unlimited Inc. announced a binding agreement to acquire Steadyhand Investment Management Ltd. and Steadyhand Investment Funds Inc., a Vancouver-based independent wealth management firm.
  • Steadyhand manages approximately $1.3 billion in assets for about 3,900 investors.
  • This acquisition is set to increase Purpose Unlimited's total assets under management to over $30 billion.
  • The deal aims to combine Steadyhand's client service focus with Purpose's technology platform and investment capabilities to enhance services for Canadian investors.
  • The transaction is subject to regulatory and unitholder approvals and is expected to close in the second quarter of 2025. Following the close, Steadyhand funds will be managed by Purpose Investments Inc., a subsidiary of Purpose Unlimited.
  • Sources: BNN Bloomberg, GlobeNewswire, Financial Post, Steadyhand Investment Funds website

2. Termination of Purpose Special Opportunities Fund (April 4, 2025)

  • Purpose Investments Inc., a division of Purpose Unlimited, announced its decision to terminate the Purpose Special Opportunities Fund by the end of 2025.
  • The decision was based on the fund's relatively low assets under management ($12.5 million as of April 3, 2025).
  • Purpose Investments has stopped accepting new purchases for the fund but will continue processing redemption requests, although some delays may occur due to the process of re-registering shares held by the fund.
  • Source: GlobeNewswire

3. March 2025 Fund Distributions Announced (Various Dates in March)

  • Purpose Investments Inc. announced its monthly distributions for March 2025 for various open-end exchange-traded funds (ETFs) and closed-end funds.
  • Specific final distribution rates for funds like the Purpose High Interest Savings Fund, Purpose US Cash Fund, Purpose Cash Management Fund, and Purpose USD Cash Management Fund were confirmed on March 26, 2025, with an ex-distribution date of March 27, 2025.
  • First-quarter 2025 distributions for the Purpose Specialty Lending Trust were also announced on March 28, 2025.
  • These are routine announcements related to the company's fund operations.
  • Sources: GlobeNewswire, Montreal Gazette

McCain Foods Ltd.

Based on the news articles from the past 30-60 days (approximately February 4, 2025, to April 5, 2025), here is a summary of recent developments concerning McCain Foods Ltd.:

Sustainability and Regenerative Agriculture:

  • Global Sustainability Report (February 27, 2025): McCain Foods released its 2024 Global Sustainability Report, detailing progress towards its sustainability goals. A key highlight is the advancement in its regenerative agriculture program.
  • Regenerative Agriculture Progress (February 27-28, 2025): The company reported significant progress in its commitment to implement regenerative agriculture practices across 100% of its potato acreage by 2030. As of the report, 71% of its farming partners globally have reached the "Onboarding" stage of the McCain Regenerative Agriculture Framework, and 24% are actively engaged in implementing these practices. McCain continues to use its "Farms of the Future" as research hubs to test and scale these sustainable farming methods.
  • Consumer Awareness Campaign (February 27-28, 2025): To educate consumers about regenerative agriculture, McCain launched the "Taste Good. Feel Good." campaign. This includes limited-edition "Regen Fries" made with potatoes from its Farm of the Future, an augmented reality game ("Farms of the Future AR"), and partnerships (including with actress Rachel Brosnahan) to raise awareness. Research cited by McCain indicates low consumer understanding of regenerative agriculture, which this campaign aims to address.
  • Sustainable Markets Initiative Project (March 24, 2025): McCain Foods, as part of the Sustainable Markets Initiative (SMI), launched a new farm project in the UK called 'Routes to Regen'. This project aims to demonstrate how cross-sector collaboration (involving food and finance businesses like McDonald's, Lloyds Banking Group, Waitrose, NatWest, etc.) can make regenerative farming more economically attractive for farmers by providing financial, technical, and peer support. The goal is to create a replicable model to accelerate the adoption of regenerative practices globally.

Other Developments:

  • Price-Fixing Lawsuit Allegations (Mentioned in articles dated February 4, 2025, referencing earlier events): While the lawsuits were filed earlier, news reports from early February 2025 discussed ongoing class-action lawsuits filed in the U.S. alleging that McCain Foods, along with Cavendish Farms, Lamb Weston, and J.R. Simplot, engaged in a price-fixing conspiracy (a "potato cartel") to raise the prices of frozen potato products starting around 2021. McCain Foods has denied these allegations and stated its intent to defend itself vigorously.

In summary, McCain Foods' recent news focuses heavily on its significant push towards regenerative agriculture, including progress updates, new initiatives with partners like the Sustainable Markets Initiative, and consumer education campaigns. Older news regarding price-fixing allegations also surfaced in reports within the last 60 days.

Partnerships & Customers:

Product & Technology Updates:

  • Web Skimmer Campaign (April 3, 2025): A sophisticated web skimmer campaign was identified using a legacy Stripe API to validate stolen payment card details before sending them to attackers.   • New Zealand BECS Direct Debit (March 31, 2025): Stripe is making New Zealand BECS Direct Debit generally available. Beta users need to update their integrations by June 30, 2025, as the API beta header will no longer be required. Stripe.js is also moving to a monthly release cycle coordinated with API releases starting March 31, 2025.
  • Stripe Insiders Forum Activity (March-April 2025): Recent discussions on the Stripe Insiders developer forum include topics like integrating UnionPay, importing products between environments, issues with customer portal tax display (EU VAT), feedback on webhook endpoints, sandboxes functionality, Xero app invoicing integration, and metered billing.

Financial & Corporate:

  • Valuation and Profitability (March 2025): Stripe reported processing $1.4 trillion in total payment volume in 2024, a 40% increase from the previous year. The company achieved profitability in 2024 and expects to remain profitable in 2025. A tender offer for employee shares valued the company at $91.5 billion, nearing its 2021 peak. Despite this, Stripe stated it has no near-term plans for an Initial Public Offering (IPO).
